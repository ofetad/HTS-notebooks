5. Make a row of boxplots of log counts of the top 5 genes where each colum shows a differnt method.

Warning: This is difficult and involves quite a bit of data processing.

In [9]:

genes.top5 <- df %>%
select(starts_with('gene')) %>%
summarize_all(mean) %>%
gather() %>%
arrange(desc(value)) %>%
head(5)
